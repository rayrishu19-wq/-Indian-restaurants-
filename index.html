Our Culinary Heritage

Since 1984, Aura has been the torchbearer of authentic Indian culinary traditions. What started as a small family kitchen in the heart of Delhi has blossomed into a sanctuary for food connoisseurs.

Our spices are hand-picked from the spice markets of Khari Baoli, and our techniques remain rooted in the royal kitchens of ancient India. At Aura, we don't just serve food; we serve stories on a plate.
